How to Activate Paramount Plus

Learn how to quickly and easily activate Paramount Plus on your device. This guide will help you understand how to sign in using an activation code and link your account on your TV or streaming device.

Step-by-Step Activation Guide

To get started with Paramount Plus on your TV or device, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Paramount Plus app on your TV or streaming device.
  2. Select the option to sign in or activate.
  3. An activation code will appear on your screen.
  4. Visit the official activation website in a browser.
  5. Enter the code, log in to your account, and confirm.
  6. Refresh your TV screen and your account will be linked.
